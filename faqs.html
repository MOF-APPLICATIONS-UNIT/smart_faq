GHANEPS (Ghana Electronic Procurement System) is a web-based, collaborative system, developed in accordance with the requirement of public procurement laws, to facilitate public procurement processes in Ghana. GHANEPS supports processes of procuring Goods, Works, Consultancy, Non-Consultancy and Disposal of assets. To qualify under Domestic Goods, the following conditions should be met;

  • Where local labour, raw materials and components from within Ghana account for 15 percent or more of the Ex-Works (EXW) price of the product offered.
  • Where imported raw materials or components constitute the bulk of the inputs and local labour and additional components are required for processing into finished products and the proportion of domestic value added is equal to 10 percent or more of the Ex Works (EXW) price of the product offered.

NB: Goods satisfying Condition (1) will attract a margin of preference of 20 percent and those meeting Condition (2) will attract 15 percent.
